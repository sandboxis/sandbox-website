Communicating with other members

Official Sandbox communications happen through Slack and email. Slack is a communications app used in teams to communicate effectively and in a structured way.

STEP 1: How to get into the group

  1. You will be invited my email
    • Not invited yet? Make sure you updated your email in the newsletter list (ask your ambassador if you are not receiving the newsletter)
  2. You will receive an invite email, use it to create your account
  3. Download the relevant slack apps
    • For your laptop/computer
    • For your iPhone, Android
    • Or for Any other device
    • If you use whatsapp/facebook/gmail on your laptop, consider trying out the Franz App
  4. Not used to slack? Read the FAQ section below to get started

STEP 2: How to get started

Once you get set up, we recommend:

  1. Set your profile picture on your profile page
  2. Set your notification preferences on your notification settings

STEP 3: Read the FAQ

Why are we using Slack?

The Facebook group is frustrating many people. It is not conducive to conversations between a large group of people. Slack allows us to separate discussions in channels and sub groups to everyone can meep track of what matters to them.

How do I use the channels?

Channels are like sub groups in the Slack. You can join any channel and leave it, set notifications for it or ignore a channel altogether.

Does this replace our hub's whatsapp/facebook/etc?

No, it does not. You are welcome to use Slack of course, but we are not trying to disturb hub-specific communication dynamics.
