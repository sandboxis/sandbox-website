Sandbox is a mobile society of trailblazers using technology and conversation to create a world of our design. We unlock your human potential.

  1. We endeavor to create positive value to our world. We are makers and doers connected by a mission to improve our local and global world.
  2. We believe that our impact is amplified by supporting the impact of others. We see the collective value of supporting one another’s efforts to create a better world.
  3. We are present with ourselves and one another. We understand the value of showing up physically and being present emotionally and intellectually.
  4. We give unconditionally. We are generous and reciprocative with our time, resources, and expertise to support the growth of all members.
  5. We strive for humility, empathy, and authenticity in our connections. We value authenticity and integrity in our relationships with one another.
  6. We respect the safety and diversity of our community. We create safe, inclusive spaces for members. Members that harm or compromise the wellbeing of other community members will be removed.
